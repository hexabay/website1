A Little History

Books are the medium to escape reality and explore the worlds beyond imagination.

Falling in love with reading books at a very young age helped me grow into a person with great imagination, and explore various situations and emotions portrayed.

It all started with Enid Blyton's books, which later expanded to classics, crime, mystery, suspense, thriller, horror, adventure, comedy and romance.

As I grew older, my love centred mostly on novels. Although I read many non-fictions like self help, motivation and auto/biographies, nothing could save me from my "novel-addiction".

Over the years I've read multiple authors and in no particular order, I have happily put them all together.
